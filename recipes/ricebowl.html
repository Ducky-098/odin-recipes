Chicken and veg rice bowl

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups of rice
  • 2 tsp of salt
  • 400g Chicken breast diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic

Method:

  1. Wash the rice by rinsing the rice and pouring out the water 3 times
  2. Put the rice into the rice cooker with equal parts water and start it on the setting of your type of rice
  3. Marinade the chicken breast in soy sauce, mirin, garlic and corn starch
  4. Heat a large non stick pan on medium high till it's ripping hot
  5. Cook the chicken till it's cooked through and remove from pan
  6. finely chop the garlic and put in the pan till it's fragrant
  7. Add whatever vegetables you prefer
  8. stir fry until the vegetables are at the desired doneness
